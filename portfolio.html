Design Standardization Leadership

The Challenge

A multi-module learning program was being developed by a team of instructional designers, but each module had inconsistent visual design and user experience. This lack of continuity was confusing learners and undermining the program's professional credibility and learning effectiveness.

My Role

As the assigned team lead, I was responsible for establishing design consistency across all modules while managing a team of instructional designers who had varying design preferences and working styles.

Approach & Solution

  • Conducted comprehensive review of existing templates and style guides with the entire ID team
  • Facilitated collaborative design sessions to identify areas where individual creativity could be maintained within brand guidelines
  • Developed a compromise framework that allowed for personal design elements while ensuring visual consistency
  • Implemented a peer review process where I evaluated all modules against established standards
  • Provided ongoing coaching and feedback to team members throughout the development process

Results & Impact

100%

Compliance with style standards achieved

25%

Reduction in future development timelines

Team Unity

Maintained team morale while achieving standards

Vendor Partnership Optimization

The Challenge

The organization partnered with an external agency to develop eLearning modules from internal storyboards, but the transition from concept to final product was resulting in significant revisions and delays. The disconnect between storyboard vision and developed modules was impacting quality, timeline, and budget.

My Role

I served as the primary liaison between our internal team and the external vendor, with responsibility for ensuring storyboards were development-ready and minimizing post-development revisions.

Approach & Solution

  • Analyzed gaps between storyboard specifications and vendor deliverables to identify common disconnect points
  • Developed enhanced storyboard standards with detailed technical specifications, visual mockups, and interaction requirements
  • Collaborated closely with SMEs to gather comprehensive screenshots, assets, and technical requirements upfront
  • Established quality checkpoints and approval processes at key development milestones
  • Created feedback loops with vendors to continuously refine the handoff process

Results & Impact

70%

Reduction in post-development revisions

On-Time

Multiple modules delivered within budget

Scalable Process

Became standard operating procedure

AI-Assisted Rapid Course Development

The Challenge

SMEs provided extensive but disorganized job aids for a manager training program on timesheet processes. Many materials were outdated, and SMEs had limited availability for traditional needs assessment processes. The project required rapid turnaround while ensuring current, relevant content.

My Role

I was responsible for efficiently analyzing the existing materials, identifying learning gaps, and developing a comprehensive course structure that would meet manager training needs without requiring extensive SME time investment.

Approach & Solution

  • Utilized AI tools to analyze and categorize existing job aids, identifying patterns and content gaps
  • Generated initial course recommendations and learning objectives based on AI analysis of materials
  • Developed streamlined SME review process focusing on validation and updates rather than ground-up content creation
  • Created detailed storyboards incorporating current best practices and SME feedback
  • Established efficient review cycles that maximized SME input while respecting their time constraints

Results & Impact

50%

Reduction in needs assessment time

High Quality

Maintained content quality and accuracy

Template Created

Process became model for future projects
